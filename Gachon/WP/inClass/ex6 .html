Computer software(often called just software) is made of one or more computer programs. Somtimes it means one specific program, or it can mean all the software on a computer, including the applications and the operating system.

Applications are programs that do a specific thing, such as a game or a word processor. The operating system(Window 7, OS X, Linux, etc.)is a software that helps tha applications run, and controls the display and the keybord.
